- **一句話定義**："Pop the hood" means to open the front cover of a car so someone can inspect the engine area.
- **為什麼存在**：This is the natural phrase a mechanic or driver may use before checking a car problem. The verb "pop" reflects the small jump or release when the hood latch opens. The same pattern appears in "pop the trunk."
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `car-repair-vocabulary`, but it is a useful standalone phrase because learners may not understand it from the words "pop" and "hood" separately.
