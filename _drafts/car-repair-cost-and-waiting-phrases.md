- **一句話定義**：Car repair cost and waiting phrases are practical sentences for asking how much a repair costs and how long the repair will take.
- **為什麼存在**：The lesson includes real repair-shop language such as "How long is this going to take?", "How much does it cost?", "Please wait here", "Your car will be ready soon", and "We will call you later." These phrases help learners manage a stressful service interaction clearly and politely.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `car-repair-vocabulary`, but this draft focuses on service interaction and time/cost negotiation rather than car parts.
