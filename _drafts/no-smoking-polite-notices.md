- **一句話定義**：No-smoking polite notices are short, calm English sentences used to tell someone that smoking is not allowed in a place.
- **為什麼存在**：Public-rule conversations need direct language that does not sound angry. The extra practice segment gives useful sentence frames such as "This is a no smoking area", "Please smoke outside", "Smoking is not allowed here", and "Thank you for understanding." These are practical in restaurants, hospitals, buses, offices, and shared buildings.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `smoking-cessation-vocabulary`, but this draft focuses on polite boundary-setting and public notices rather than smoker vocabulary or habit change.
