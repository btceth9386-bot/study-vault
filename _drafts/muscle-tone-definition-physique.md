- **一句話定義**：Muscle tone, definition, and physique are English terms for describing the shape, visibility, and overall appearance of the body.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode contrasts different fitness goals: developing strength, improving muscle tone, seeing definition, and having a good physique. These words are more precise than simply saying "fit" or "strong" and help learners describe what kind of body result they want from exercise.
