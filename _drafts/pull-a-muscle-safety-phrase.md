- **一句話定義**："Pull a muscle" means to hurt a muscle by stretching or using it too much.
- **為什麼存在**：The phrase is common in exercise, sports, running, and workplace injury conversations. The episode uses it as a safety warning: stretch before pumping iron or you might pull a muscle. It helps learners talk about minor injuries without overusing the more serious phrase "tear a muscle."
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `gym-workout-vocabulary` because it appears in a gym safety context, but it also transfers to general health and sports vocabulary.
