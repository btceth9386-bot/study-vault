- **一句話定義**："Die on me" means that a machine, vehicle, device, or sometimes a person stopped working or failed while I was using or depending on it.
- **為什麼存在**：This phrase is common when reporting sudden failure: a car died on me, my phone is going to die on me, or my CD player died on me. It adds the nuance that the failure happened during use, creating inconvenience or urgency.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `busted-informal-broken` and `car-repair-vocabulary`, but it focuses on failure during use rather than the general state of being broken.
