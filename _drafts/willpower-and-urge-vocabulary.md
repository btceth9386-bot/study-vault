- **一句話定義**："Willpower" is self-control used to follow a decision, while an "urge" is a strong desire or impulse to do something.
- **為什麼存在**：These words help learners describe the inner struggle behind habits and temptations. In the episode, the smoker says she lacks the willpower to fight the urge to reach for cigarettes. The same vocabulary works for dieting, drinking, shopping, procrastination, and other self-control situations.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `cold-turkey-idiom` and `cut-down-vs-quit` because all three describe habit change. It is separate because this draft focuses on mental state and impulse vocabulary rather than the action strategy.
