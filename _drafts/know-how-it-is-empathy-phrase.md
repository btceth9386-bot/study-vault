- **一句話定義**："I know how it is" is a conversational empathy phrase meaning "I understand your situation because I have experienced something similar."
- **為什麼存在**：The phrase lets speakers show sympathy naturally without overexplaining. In the episode, it reassures a newcomer who does not know where to begin at the gym. It also works for new jobs, new schools, confusing systems, and other situations where someone feels lost.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `cut-me-some-slack-idiom` because both soften social interaction, but it is not a merge candidate because this phrase expresses understanding rather than asking for leniency.
