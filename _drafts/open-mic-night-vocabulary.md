- **一句話定義**：An open mic night is a live event where non-professional or emerging performers can take the microphone and perform for an audience.
- **為什麼存在**：This phrase appears in music, comedy, poetry, and bar or cafe culture. The episode uses it as the setting for a comedy routine, but the concept is broader: it names a venue format where the stage is open to amateurs, learners, or local performers.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `stand-up-comedy-vocabulary` because open mic night is a common stand-up setting. It is a separate draft because open mic events can involve many performance types, not only comedy.
