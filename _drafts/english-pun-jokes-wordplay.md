- **一句話定義**：English pun jokes use similar sounds, homophones, or double meanings to create humor.
- **為什麼存在**：Many jokes are impossible to understand from dictionary meanings alone. The episode explains jokes that depend on sound patterns, such as "movies" echoing "moo-vies" and "dam" sounding like "damn". This teaches learners to listen for pronunciation and double meaning when interpreting humor.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to English vocabulary concepts because it depends on word meaning and pronunciation, but it is distinct because the core skill is recognizing humor through sound and ambiguity.
