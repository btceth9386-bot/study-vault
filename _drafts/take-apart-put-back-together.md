- **一句話定義**："Take apart" means to remove the pieces of something, while "put back together" means to reassemble those pieces.
- **為什麼存在**：The mechanic says the repair requires taking apart several systems and putting them back together. These phrases are useful beyond cars: children take apart toys, people take apart clocks or gadgets, and repair manuals show how to reassemble devices.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `car-repair-vocabulary`, but it also connects to general repair, tools, and troubleshooting language.
