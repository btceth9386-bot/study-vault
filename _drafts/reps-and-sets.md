- **一句話定義**：A "rep" is one repetition of an exercise, while a "set" is a group of reps performed before resting.
- **為什麼存在**：Reps and sets are the basic counting system for weight training and exercise plans. In the episode, the trainer recommends 10 to 15 reps in four sets, which means each movement is repeated several times and grouped into rounds. Without this distinction, gym instructions are easy to misunderstand.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `gym-workout-vocabulary` because it is part of the same fitness domain, but it is specific enough to deserve its own reviewable concept.
