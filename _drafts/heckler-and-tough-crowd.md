- **一句話定義**：A "heckler" is someone who interrupts or criticizes a performer from the audience, while a "tough crowd" is an audience that is hard to please.
- **為什麼存在**：These phrases help learners describe negative audience reactions in comedy, presentations, meetings, and public speaking. The episode shows a comic facing boos and poor audience response, which makes the vocabulary memorable and transferable.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `stand-up-comedy-vocabulary` and `open-mic-night-vocabulary`, but it focuses specifically on audience behavior and speaker pressure.
