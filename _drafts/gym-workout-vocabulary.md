- **一句話定義**：Gym workout vocabulary is the practical set of English words used to describe exercise places, machines, routines, and basic training activities.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode teaches words and phrases such as gym, work out, pump iron, treadmill, weights, cardio, stretch, and training. These terms let learners ask for help, describe routines, follow trainer instructions, and talk about healthy habits in everyday English.
