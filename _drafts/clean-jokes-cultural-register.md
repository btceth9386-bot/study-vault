- **一句話定義**：A "clean joke" is humor that avoids swearing, sexual content, or offensive material so it is safer for public, classroom, or workplace settings.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode ends by asking listeners to share jokes but keep them clean, which highlights that humor has register and audience constraints. English learners need this because a joke can be grammatically correct but socially inappropriate in the wrong context.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `english-pun-jokes-wordplay` because both explain humor, but this draft focuses on appropriateness and setting rather than the mechanics of the joke.
