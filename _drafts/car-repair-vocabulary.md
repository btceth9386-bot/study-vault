- **一句話定義**：Car repair vocabulary is the practical English used to describe car problems, car parts, and repair-shop interactions.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode teaches words such as mechanic, radiator, pistons, transmission, fuel pump, spare parts, engine, tire, oil change, and repair shop. These words matter because car trouble is stressful and learners need clear language to explain the problem, understand the mechanic, and ask about time or cost.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `construction-tools-vocabulary` because both involve practical mechanical vocabulary, but this is not a duplicate because it focuses on vehicles and repair shops.
