- **一句話定義**：Stand-up comedy vocabulary is the set of English words used to describe a live comedy performance, the performer, the prepared jokes, and the audience response.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode teaches words such as "comic", "stand-up comedy", "routine", and "zinger", which learners need to understand entertainment conversations and cultural references. These words also help learners talk about public speaking, performance quality, and prepared material.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to existing English vocabulary concepts because it groups a practical domain, but it is not a duplicate of existing work, supermarket, or construction vocabulary.
