- **一句話定義**："Busted" is informal English for broken, damaged, or not working.
- **為什麼存在**：The episode uses "busted" for broken car parts, but the word also applies to phones, DVD players, arms, ribs, windows, and vases. It helps learners recognize casual native speech where people often say "it's busted" instead of "it is broken."
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `car-repair-vocabulary`, but it is broader than cars because it can describe many broken objects or body parts.
