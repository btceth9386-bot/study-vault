- **一句話定義**："Ripped" is an informal adjective meaning very muscular with clearly visible muscles.
- **為什麼存在**：Learners may know "rip" as a verb meaning tear, but the episode teaches "ripped" as gym slang for a highly muscular body. It is useful in casual conversation, fitness media, and compliments, but it is more informal than terms like muscular or well-built.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to `muscle-tone-definition-physique`, but it should stay separate because it is slang with a specific social tone.
