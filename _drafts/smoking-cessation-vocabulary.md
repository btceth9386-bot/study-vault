- **一句話定義**：Smoking cessation vocabulary is the set of English words and phrases used to talk about smoking, reducing smoking, quitting, and smoking-related environments.
- **為什麼存在**：The lesson uses common everyday terms such as "pack of smokes", "ashtray", and "light up" that learners may hear in casual conversation, public rules, health discussions, or family conflict. Knowing these terms helps learners understand both literal smoking situations and broader habit-change conversations.
- **與既有概念的關聯**：Related to existing English vocabulary concepts such as `supermarket-checkout-vocabulary` and `construction-tools-vocabulary` because it groups a domain-specific set of words from one practical conversation, but it is not a duplicate because the domain is smoking and public health.
