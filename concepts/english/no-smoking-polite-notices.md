No Smoking Polite Notices is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. No-smoking polite notices are short, calm English sentences used to tell someone that smoking is not allowed in a place. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. Public-rule conversations need direct language that does not sound angry.
