# Busted as Informal Broken

- **一句話定義**："Busted" is informal English for broken, damaged, or not working.

## 摘要

Busted as Informal Broken is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. "Busted" is informal English for broken, damaged, or not working. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. The episode uses "busted" for broken car parts, but the word also applies to phones, DVD players, arms, ribs, windows, and vases.
