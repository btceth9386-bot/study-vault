Take Apart and Put Back Together is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. "Take apart" means to remove the pieces of something, while "put back together" means to reassemble those pieces. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. The mechanic says the repair requires taking apart several systems and putting them back together.
