## 摘要

I Know How It Is is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. "I know how it is" is a conversational empathy phrase meaning "I understand your situation because I have experienced something similar.". The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. The phrase lets speakers show sympathy naturally without overexplaining.
