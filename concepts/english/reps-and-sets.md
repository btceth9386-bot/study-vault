# Reps and Sets

- **一句話定義**：A "rep" is one repetition of an exercise, while a "set" is a group of reps performed before resting.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：Reps and sets are the basic counting system for weight training and exercise plans. In the episode, the trainer recommends 10 to 15 reps in four sets, which means each movement is repeated several times and grouped into rounds. Without this distinction, gym instructions are easy to misunderstand.
