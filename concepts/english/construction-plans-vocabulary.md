# Construction Plans Vocabulary

- **One-sentence definition**: Construction **plans** are the drawings or design map that show how a building project should be built.
- **Why it exists / what problem it solves**: "Plans" can mean general intentions, but in construction it means technical drawings such as floor plans. This meaning helps learners understand instructions like "follow the plans exactly."
- **Keywords**: plans, floor plans, drawings, design map, construction, instructions

## Summary

Construction plans are like a map for a building project. They tell workers what to build, where parts should go, and how the finished structure should look. In the lesson, the workers have already reviewed the plans before starting the deck.

The key language point is context. "I have plans tonight" means personal intentions. "Follow the plans exactly" on a job site means follow the technical drawings.

## Example

> "We've been over the plans, but we really need to make sure everything is up to code."

Other examples:

> "The architect is still drawing the floor plans."
> "These kitchen plans need to be translated before construction starts."
