# Idiom: Go Cold Turkey

- **一句話定義**："Go cold turkey" means to stop a habit suddenly and completely, without gradually reducing it.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：This idiom appears often in conversations about smoking, alcohol, sugar, social media, and other habits. It gives a concise way to say that someone stopped all at once instead of slowly cutting down, and it often implies that the change is difficult.
