# Phrasal Verb: Crack Down On

- **一句話定義**："Crack down on" means to become stricter about stopping, limiting, or punishing a behavior.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：The phrase is common in news, law, school, workplace, and public-policy contexts. In the episode, smoking laws are "cracking down" by limiting where people can smoke. The same phrase can describe police cracking down on drunk driving, schools cracking down on lateness, or companies cracking down on policy violations.
