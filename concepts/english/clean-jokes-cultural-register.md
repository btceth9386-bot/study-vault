## 摘要

Clean Jokes and Cultural Register is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. A "clean joke" is humor that avoids swearing, sexual content, or offensive material so it is safer for public, classroom, or workplace settings. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. The episode ends by asking listeners to share jokes but keep them clean, which highlights that humor has register and audience constraints.
