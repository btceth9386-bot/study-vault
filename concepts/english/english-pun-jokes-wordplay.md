# English Pun Jokes and Wordplay

- **一句話定義**：English pun jokes use similar sounds, homophones, or double meanings to create humor.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：Many jokes are impossible to understand from dictionary meanings alone. The episode explains jokes that depend on sound patterns, such as "movies" echoing "moo-vies" and "dam" sounding like "damn". ## 摘要

English Pun Jokes and Wordplay is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. English pun jokes use similar sounds, homophones, or double meanings to create humor. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. Many jokes are impossible to understand from dictionary meanings alone.
