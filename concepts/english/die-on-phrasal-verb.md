# Phrasal Verb: Die On Me

- **一句話定義**："Die on me" means that a machine, vehicle, device, or sometimes a person stopped working or failed while I was using or depending on it.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：This phrase is common when reporting sudden failure: a car died on me, my phone is going to die on me, or my CD player died on me. It adds the nuance that the failure happened during use, creating inconvenience or urgency.

## 摘要

Die On Me is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. "Die on me" means that a machine, vehicle, device, or sometimes a person stopped working or failed while I was using or depending on it. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. This phrase is common when reporting sudden failure: a car died on me, my phone is going to die on me, or my CD player died on me.
