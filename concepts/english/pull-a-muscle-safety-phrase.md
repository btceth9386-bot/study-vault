# Pull a Muscle Safety Phrase

- **一句話定義**："Pull a muscle" means to hurt a muscle by stretching or using it too much.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：The phrase is common in exercise, sports, running, and workplace injury conversations. The episode uses it as a safety warning: stretch before pumping iron or you might pull a muscle. It helps learners talk about minor injuries without overusing the more serious phrase "tear a muscle."

## 摘要

Pull a Muscle Safety Phrase is a practical English pattern you can recognize and reuse in real conversations. "Pull a muscle" means to hurt a muscle by stretching or using it too much. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. The phrase is common in exercise, sports, running, and workplace injury conversations.
