# Pop the Hood

- **一句話定義**："Pop the hood" means to open the front cover of a car so someone can inspect the engine area.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：This is the natural phrase a mechanic or driver may use before checking a car problem. The verb "pop" reflects the small jump or release when the hood latch opens. The same pattern appears in "pop the trunk."
