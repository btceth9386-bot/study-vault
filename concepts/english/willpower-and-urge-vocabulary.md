# Willpower and Urge Vocabulary

- **一句話定義**："Willpower" is self-control used to follow a decision, while an "urge" is a strong desire or impulse to do something.
- **為什麼存在 / 解決什麼問題**：These words help learners describe the inner struggle behind habits and temptations. The important point is not only the dictionary meaning, but when a speaker would naturally choose it. These words help learners describe the inner struggle behind habits and temptations.
